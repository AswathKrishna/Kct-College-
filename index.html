KCT-COUNSELLING

C O U N S E L L I N G

Counseling for personal well-being is yet another active forum in KCT where the student guidance
process takes place in a one-to-one, and confidential environment.The counselor, who is highly
empathetic assists the students in the areas of their need. Students are guided on different
perspectives and with a non-judgemental attitude thus leading them to find a solution on their own.

C O U N S E L L O R

Vallikala Sugavaneswaran

A Psychologist by profession with Transactional Analysis as base.She has been a senior facilitator in a Montessori institution dealing with peers,parents and the kids for twelve years. At present she deals with adolescents as a student counselor promoting emotional well being within our campus.
